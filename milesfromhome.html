About the Book

Miles From Home is a travel memoir about stepping beyond familiar borders and discovering that home is not always a place—it is something carried within us. Through airports, train stations, ancient cities, sacred sites, and unexpected encounters, Halaina shares the experiences that changed the way she saw the world and herself.

Excerpt

I remember airports more clearly than I remember most people. Not one airport specifically. All of them. London. Paris. Madrid. Dublin. Frankfurt. Long terminals beneath fluorescent lights. Rolling suitcases across polished floors. Coffee brewing before sunrise while exhausted travelers searched for gates and direction. Every airport felt temporary. That was why I loved them.
