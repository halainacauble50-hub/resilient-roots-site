Resilient Roots

About the Book

Resilient Roots is a powerful memoir about survival, faith, family, and the courage to begin again. From childhood in Ramallah to rebuilding life in the United States, Halaina Cauble tells a story of loss, resilience, and the quiet strength it takes to reclaim your life.

About the Author

Halaina Cauble is the author of Resilient Roots, a literary memoir tracing her journey from survival to self-reclamation. A mother of four, entrepreneur, and salon owner, she rebuilt her life after abuse, betrayal, financial loss, and displacement—emerging not hardened, but grounded. She lives in South Carolina, where she continues to write, travel, and build spaces rooted in connection and strength.
