About the Book

Inherited Strength is a deeply personal memoir about the women who came before us, the burdens we inherit, and the choices that shape who we become. Through family stories, motherhood, loss, resilience, and reinvention, Halaina explores what is passed from one generation to the next—and what must finally be released. At its heart, it is a story of healing, self-discovery, and finding strength in your own voice.
