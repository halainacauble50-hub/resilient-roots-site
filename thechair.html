Set inside a small-town salon, The Chair follows the lives of women whose stories unfold one appointment at a time. Secrets, grief, forgiveness, love, and second chances all pass through Claire's salon chair.

As Claire helps others carry their burdens, a message from her own past threatens to uncover the story she has spent years trying to leave behind.
