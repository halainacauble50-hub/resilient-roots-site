Miles From Home

A memoir of travel, transformation, and finding home in the world.

View on Amazon

For years I traveled alone with two suitcases, a passport, and a growing curiosity about the world. From Spain to Ireland, London to Morocco, Israel to Hungary, each journey became part of a larger story—not just about places, but about becoming.

Packed with personal experiences, travel tips, sightseeing recommendations, local history, and cultural insights, Miles From Home is both a memoir and a travel companion.

Each chapter introduces memorable destinations, hidden gems, practical travel advice, and fascinating historical and cultural background that bring every location to life.

Whether you're planning your next adventure or simply exploring from your favorite chair, Miles From Home offers inspiration, knowledge, and unforgettable stories for travelers, dreamers, and lifelong learners alike.

Excerpt

I remember airports more clearly than I remember most people. Not one airport specifically. All of them. London. Paris. Madrid. Dublin. Frankfurt. Long terminals beneath fluorescent lights. Rolling suitcases across polished floors. Coffee brewing before sunrise while exhausted travelers searched for gates and direction. Every airport felt temporary. That was why I loved them.
