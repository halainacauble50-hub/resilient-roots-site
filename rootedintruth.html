Rooted In Truth

A Writer's Guide to Finding, Facing, and Telling Your Story.

View on Amazon Back to Main Website

Every story begins with a memory. Rooted In Truth is a guide for writers who want to transform life experiences into meaningful, compelling books. Drawing from her own publishing journey, Halaina shares lessons on voice, structure, honesty, and the courage required to tell the truth.

Who This Book Is For

• Memoir writers
• First-time authors
• Journal keepers
• Family historians
• Anyone with a story they feel called to tell

About the Book

Rooted In Truth takes readers behind the scenes of writing a memoir—from the first memory to the final published page. It explores fear, self-doubt, editing, structure, and the emotional challenge of putting personal experiences into words.

Part writing guide and part personal reflection, this book offers practical advice while encouraging writers to embrace authenticity and trust their own voice.

What You'll Learn

• Finding your authentic voice
• Organizing memories into chapters
• Writing scenes that feel alive
• Handling difficult personal material
• Editing without losing emotion
• Preparing a manuscript for publication

Excerpt

Every book begins the same way. Not with a chapter. Not with a title. Not even with an outline. It begins with something that refuses to leave you alone. A memory. A question. A moment that keeps returning long after everything else has faded. The writer's task is not to invent meaning. The task is to discover it.
