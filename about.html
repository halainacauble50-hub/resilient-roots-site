About Halaina Cauble

Halaina Cauble

Halaina Cauble is the author of Resilient Roots, Miles From Home, Rooted in Truth, One Land, and Two Sides. A mother of four, entrepreneur, salon owner, and traveler, she writes stories rooted in faith, family, resilience, history, and the courage to begin again.

Living in South Carolina, Halaina continues to write stories that inspire hope, healing, and perseverance while encouraging readers to find strength through life's greatest challenges.

My Mission

My goal is to write stories that remind people they are stronger than they realize. Whether through memoir or historical fiction, I hope every reader closes one of my books with renewed faith, hope, and courage.
