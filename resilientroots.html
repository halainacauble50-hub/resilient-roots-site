A Memoir of Survival, Faith, and the Courage to Begin Again

Resilient Roots is the true story of a young girl born in Ramallah whose life was forever shaped by war, displacement, faith, and the unbreakable bonds of family.

Through unimaginable loss and extraordinary resilience, Halaina Cauble shares the journey that carried her from the Middle East to rebuilding a new life in America.

More than a memoir, Resilient Roots is a story of hope, courage, forgiveness, and discovering that our deepest roots are often planted during life's greatest storms.

Book Description

Through hardship, motherhood, immigration, faith, and personal transformation, Resilient Roots explores what survives when everything else changes.

This memoir is a story of family, identity, resilience, and finding the strength to rebuild a life from the ground up.
